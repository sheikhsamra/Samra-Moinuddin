About Me

Every journey begins with a spark. Mine started with a simple curiosity: “How are websites made?” What began as interest slowly grew into passion, and eventually into a mission.

Hi, I’m Sheikh Samra Moinuddin

I am a Frontend Developer, UI/UX Designer, Graphic Designer, and an explorer of cutting-edge technologies. I still remember the first time I wrote a simple HTML tag and saw a tiny piece of text appear on the screen. That moment taught me something powerful: technology is magic — and I wanted to master that magic.

As a naturally creative person, design has never been just about colors or shapes — it’s about emotion, storytelling, and identity. When I combined design with coding, I realized: My strength isn’t just writing code — it's creating meaningful digital experiences.

Over time, I gained strong command over HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, React, Next.js, TailwindCSS, Bootstrap, Firebase, Python, and AI tools — especially Agentic AI, which I am actively exploring. Each project challenged me, shaped me, and helped me grow into a more confident professional.

Today, I don’t just build websites — I craft digital experiences. Experiences that are fast, visually appealing, intuitive, and human-centered. Whether it’s an e-commerce platform, a portfolio, a modern UI, an AI-powered application, or a custom interactive interface — my goal is always the same: “Perfection. Performance. Personality.”

I value every pixel, every animation, every spacing, and every interaction — because every detail is part of the story I create. With multiple successful projects under my belt, my love for technology continues to grow. Each new project is an opportunity to build something unique.

If you are looking for someone who can think creatively, design beautifully, develop intelligently, and deliver professionally — I would love to bring your ideas to life. Because for me: “It’s not just about building a website. It’s about creating an experience people remember.”
